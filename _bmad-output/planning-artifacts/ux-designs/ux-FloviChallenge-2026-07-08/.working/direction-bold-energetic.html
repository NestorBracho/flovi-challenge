Direction 03 · bold-energetic

Channels modern gig-economy consumer apps: big confident type, a single vibrant accent against near-black and white, and high-contrast CTAs that make the next action unmissable. It's tuned for the driver's browse-and-book moment, where someone is scanning gigs fast and "Book this gig" needs to read as an obvious, satisfying tap. Applied to the dispatcher dashboard it trades data density for clarity and speed — more lightweight ops tool than enterprise console, which may be a feature or a gap depending on how many requests a dispatcher juggles at once.
